My Plan My Business Model

    If I really believe Salt Lake City can become the new Nashville, I need to help you see the same. You see, everytime shows like NBC's The Voice or American Idol hold auditions, they always talk about Utah being their "gold mine". This is because of the insane amount of talent here, but if this is the case, why are we not seeing more Utah artists making it "big" in the industry? This is because there is almost no industry in Utah. Musicians often feel the need to leave Utah in order to find success. My plan is to change that. I want to teach artists how to be successful and to help them accomplish their dreams.

    My business model is as follows; Artist —> Us —> Artist —> The World

    Artist will come to us with their projects. For example, I want to release two songs, and a music video. I would like help getting it on streaming platforms, promoting it on social media, and booking live shows. Our job is then to help the artist with all their wants and needs. We want to help you be successful, we want to see you fulfill your dreams and we are here for the ride.
